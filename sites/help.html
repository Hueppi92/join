Help

Welcome to the Help section. Here you can find information and assistance on how to use the Kanban Project Management Tool effectively.

Getting Started

To get started, log in to your account or sign up if you are a new user. Once logged in, you can create tasks, manage your project board, and collaborate with team members.

Using the Task Board

The task board is divided into several columns representing different stages of task completion. You can add new tasks, move them between columns, and update their status as needed.

Managing Tasks

To create a new task, navigate to the task editor and fill in the required details such as task name, description, due date, and assignees. You can also add subtasks to break down larger tasks into manageable parts.

Collaboration

Invite team members to your project to collaborate on tasks. You can assign tasks to specific members and track their progress on the task board.

Support

If you need further assistance, please contact our support team at support@kanbanprojecttool.com or visit our FAQ page.
